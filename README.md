Based on the analysis, here are the top findings:
1.  **Rush Hour Trends:** Sales peak significantly at **7:00 PM**, with secondary spikes at 1:00 PM and 3:00 PM.
2.  **Payment Methods:** Customers prefer **E-wallets** and **Cash** almost equally, while Credit Card usage lags behind.
3.  **Demographic Spending:** **Members** have a higher average transaction value ($327) than non-members ($318).
4.  **Category breakdown by Gender:** Male customers generated the highest revenue in the **Health & Beauty** sector, while Female customers dominated **Food & Beverages**.
